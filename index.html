Barky - Navigation, Barcode Style

This little library will automagically replace your anchor links with barcode equivilants. Blip the barcode with your scanner to navigation. No more nasty mouses!

  • Universal CODE-128 barcodes
  • Up to 69 barcodes on a page[1]
  • Use onclick to fire javascript actions from barcodes

Built atop Johan Lindell's excellent JsBarcode.

Tell me!

Barky.js looks through your page for links with the correct class. These links are then replaced with an ASCII codepoint between 58 (;) and 127 (DELETE) - hence the 69 limit. Barky keeps a lookup table in JS mapping codepoints to URLs. When you scan a barcode, Barky intercepts the keypress, looks up the URL and sends you to it.
