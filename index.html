Barky - Navigation, Barcode Style

This little library will automagically replace your anchor links with barcode equivilants. Blip the barcode with your scanner to navigation. No more nasty mouses!

  • Universal CODE-128 barcodes
  • Up to 69 barcodes on a page
  • Use onclick to fire javascript actions from barcodes
  • Barcode length doesn't depend on URL size
